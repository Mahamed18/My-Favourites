Countryballs

I've always been really into geography, but one day I stumbled across a unique type of comics called Countryballs. They are comics where each country is you guessed it in ball form. The comics are typically filled to the brim with references to things in geopolitics both currently and in history. There are a lot of channels who use these as visuals while disussing politics and history. Here are my favourites:

  • Kraut - I recommend this channel the most as everything discussed is very well made and in depth. Most of his content speaks about how countries ended up in the situation their in now. My personal favourite series from him is where he compares Mexico and the US and why the US is far richer. Despite him being very well versed in english literature he is from Vienna, Austria.
  • Hoser - He has actually strayed away from the Countryball format I'm not entirely sure why but his content is mainly focused on current events around the world and his predictions. Plus he is a fellow Canadian so that's cool!
  • Icariaball - This channel is actually mainly comedic and pokes fun at current and past events around the world. His "Meet the ____" series is my personal favourite as it has the most references and I find it fun to be able to point out the little details and understand them. (Also, he is from Greece and his video thumbnail is the image used above).
